Welcome friends.

Cyber Wizard Institute (CWI) is a mobile collaborative programming school that focuses on modular, Unix-style software development.

We are running a session in Prishtina.

Date: 5 to 23 June
Location: Prishtina Hackerspace

CWI is open to anyone who wants to learn programming, regardless of present ability; even complete novices are welcome. It is free of charge. Apply by June 1.

We previously ran two sessions of CWI in 2015 in Sudo Room hackerspace in Oakland, CA.
