Welcome friends.

Cyber Wizard Institute (CWI) is a mobile collaborative programming school that focuses on modular, Unix-style software development.

We are running a session in Prishtina.

Date: 5 to 23 June
Location: Prishtina Hackerspace
Application deadline: June 1st (apply)

CWI is open to anyone who wants to learn programming, regardless of present ability; even complete novices are welcome. It is free of charge. We previously ran two sessions of CWI in 2015 in Sudo Room hackerspace in Oakland, CA.

In case you have any questions, please write to us at cwi@flossk.org
